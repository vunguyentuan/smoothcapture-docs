## **Minimum requirements**

<Card href="/quickstart">
  To ensure optimal performance when using **Smooth Capture**, your Mac should meet the following requirements:

  ### Operating System

  - **macOS Sequoia 15.2** or later

  ### Processor

  One of the following:

  - **Apple M1 or later** (recommended)
  - **Intel-based Mac** from these models:
    - MacBook Pro (2018 or later)
    - MacBook Air (2020 or later)

  ### Memory

  - **8 GB RAM minimum**

  **Note:** Performance may vary depending on your recording needs. For the best experience across all recording types, we recommend using a Mac with an **Apple M1 processor or later**.
</Card>

## Checking Your Mac Specifications

To verify whether your Mac meets the requirements:

1. Click the **Apple** menu in the top-left corner of your screen.
2. Select **About This Mac**.
3. Review the following information:
   - macOS version
   - Processor type
   - Memory (RAM)

The **About This Mac** window displays all the details needed to compare your system with the requirements above.
