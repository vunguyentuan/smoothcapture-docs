**Install Smooth Capture on macOS**

Smooth Capture is a desktop app exclusively for **macOS**.

<Card horizontal={false} href="/quickstart">
  1. Download the app from:\
     https://download.smoothcapture.app/SmoothCapture-latest.dmg
  2. Once you click the download link, the download will start automatically.
  3. Open the downloaded `.dmg` file.
  4. When prompted: Drag the **Smooth Capture** icon into the **Applications** folder.
  5. Smooth Capture will be installed and available in the **Applications** folder on your Mac.
</Card>
