### Add manual zoom

Manual Zoom lets you control exactly **where the camera focuses**, instead of relying on clicks or automatic tracking.

Use it when:

- You want to highlight a specific area
- There’s no mouse click to trigger zoom
- You need precise control over framing

## How to Use

1. **Create or select a zoom**
    - Add a new zoom segment on the timeline
    - Or click an existing zoom block
2. **Switch to Manual mode**
    - In the right panel, choose **Manual Zoom**
3. **Set focus point**
    - A preview will appear with a **purple dot (focus point)**
    - Drag it to the area you want to zoom into
4. **Adjust zoom level**
    - Increase or decrease the zoom scale to control how close it gets

## Tips

- Use Manual Zoom for **intentional storytelling**, not just random zooms
- Keep the focus near **key UI elements** (buttons, inputs, results)
- Avoid over-zooming → it looks amateur fast
