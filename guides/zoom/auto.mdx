## Auto Zoom

Auto Zoom automatically focuses on **where you click** during your recording — no manual setup needed.

---

## How It Works

- SmoothCapture detects **mouse clicks** in your recording
- Each zoom will **center on the click position**
- The camera smoothly zooms in to highlight that interaction

---

## When to Use

Auto Zoom is ideal when:

- You’re doing **click-based demos**
- You want fast editing with **minimal setup**
- Most interactions are driven by mouse input

---

## Important

- Auto Zoom **only works if clicks exist** in that segment
- No clicks → **no zoom will be applied**

In that case, switch to **Manual Zoom** and choose the focus area yourself.

---

## Tip

If your recording feels “dead” or unfocused, it’s usually because:

- You used Auto Zoom
- But there were **no clicks to guide it**

Don’t guess—use Manual Zoom instead.
