## Lightbox Effect

Lightbox helps you **highlight what matters** while softly blurring everything else.

Use it to:
- Guide attention to a specific area
- Make your demo feel more intentional and polished

<video controls className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl" src="/videos/lightbox-effect.mp4" />

## How to Use

1. **Open your recording** in the editor
2. **Enable Effect Track**
3. Turn on **Lightbox** in the right panel
4. Click **Edit Selection** to choose the focus area
5. Position it on the timeline where needed

---

<video controls className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl" src="/videos/use-lightbox-effect.mp4" />

## Adjust Settings

- **Size** → controls how large the highlighted area is
- **Background Blur** → controls how strong the blur is

---

## Important

- Lightbox can span across your timeline
- But **only one Lightbox can exist at a time per frame**

## Why Use Lightbox

Lightbox isn’t just visual polish — it fixes a real problem:

Most demos fail because viewers don’t know **where to look**.

Use Lightbox to:

- Direct attention instantly
- Reduce visual noise
- Make your recording feel **premium, not chaotic**
