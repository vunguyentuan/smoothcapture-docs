### Adjust Segment Speed

You can change the speed in either of the following ways:

- **Select a segment**\
  Click on the segment in the timeline, then adjust **Segment Speed** in the right-hand panel.

![SCR 20260108 Nmwm](/images/SCR-20260108-nmwm.png)

- **Right-click the segment**\
  Right-click on a segment and select **Speed**.

![SCR 20260108 Nnnv](/images/SCR-20260108-nnnv.png)
