---
title: "Mouse click sound"
---

Adding a mouse click sound effect can make your recording feel more polished and easier to follow.

<video controls className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl" src="/videos/mouse-sound.mp4" />

Follow these steps to add a click sound:

1. In the editor, open the **Cursor** tab on the right side.
2. Scroll down to the **Click Sound** section.
3. Choose a mouse click sound effect from the available options.\
   You can preview each sound to find the one that fits your project.
4. Adjust the **volume** so the click sound blends naturally with your other audio.
5. Preview your video to make sure the effect enhances the viewing experience without overpowering other sounds.
