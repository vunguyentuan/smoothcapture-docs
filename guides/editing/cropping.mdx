## How to Use Crop

You can use the Crop tool to adjust the visible area of your recording.

![Download](/images/download.png)

1. In the editor, click the **Crop** button above the recording preview.
2. The **Crop Tool** window will appear.
3. Adjust the crop by:
   - Dragging the edges of the crop area manually, or
   - Selecting a preset **aspect ratio** for the crop.
