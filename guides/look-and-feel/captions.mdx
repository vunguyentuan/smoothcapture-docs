## Captions (Subtitles)

Captions help make your videos more accessible and easier to follow, especially when watched without sound. SmoothCapture allows you to generate multilingual captions for your videos, All captions are created based on your voiceover.

![SCR 20260108 Jtfq](/images/SCR-20260108-jtfq.png)

### Show Subtitles

- **Show Subtitles**\
  Toggle this on to display captions in your video.

---

### Subtitle Size

- **Subtitle Size**\
  Adjust the size of the captions to suit your video layout and resolution.

---

### Audio Source

- **Source**\
  Choose which audio track to use for generating subtitles (for example, **Screen Recording**).

---

### Transcription Engine

- **Engine**\
  Select the speech recognition engine used to generate captions (for example, **Apple Speech**).

---

### Language

- **Language**\
  Choose the spoken language of your audio to improve transcription accuracy.

---

![SCR 20260108 Jtuq](/images/SCR-20260108-jtuq.png)

### Transcription

- **Transcribe Audio**\
  Click this button to generate captions from your selected audio source.

Once transcription is complete, subtitles will be created automatically.

---

### Transcript Editor

- **Transcript**\
  View and edit the generated transcript. Any changes you make are saved automatically and reflected in the subtitles.

If no transcript is available, click **Transcribe Audio** to generate one.
