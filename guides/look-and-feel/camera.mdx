---
title: "Camera (Webcam Overlay)"
---

The webcam overlay lets you add your camera feed on top of your screen recording, making your videos more personal and engaging.

### Enable Webcam

- **Enable Webcam**\
  Toggle this on to display your camera during recording.

---

### Position

Choose where the webcam overlay appears on your video.\
Select one of the preset positions to place the camera feed in the corner or edge that works best for your layout.

---
