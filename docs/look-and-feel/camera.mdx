---
title: "Camera (Webcam Overlay)"
---

The webcam overlay lets you add your camera feed on top of your screen recording, making your videos more personal and engaging.

![SCR 20260108 Jonh](/images/SCR-20260108-jonh.png)

### Enable Webcam

- **Enable Webcam**\
  Toggle this on to display your camera during recording.

---

### Position

Choose where the webcam overlay appears on your video.\
Select one of the preset positions to place the camera feed in the corner or edge that works best for your layout.

---

### Size & Shape

- **Size**\
  Adjust how large the webcam overlay appears in your video.
- **Shape**
  - **Square**: Cropped square layout for a clean, modern look.
  - **Original**: Keeps the cameraâ€™s original aspect ratio.
- **Roundness**\
  Controls how rounded the corners of the webcam overlay appear.
- **Edge Gap**\
  Adjusts the spacing between the webcam overlay and the edge of the video frame

![SCR 20260108 Jpmh](/images/SCR-20260108-jpmh.png)

### Effects

- **Shadow**\
  Adds a soft shadow behind the webcam overlay to create depth.
- **Shadow Intensity**\
  Controls how strong the shadow appears.
- **Border**\
  Adds a border around the webcam overlay.
- **Border Width**\
  Adjusts the thickness of the border.
