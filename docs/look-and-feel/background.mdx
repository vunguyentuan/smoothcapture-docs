### Customize Your Background

You can enhance the look of your screencast by placing it on a background of your choice.

To select a background and adjust its settings, open **Background** in the editor settings on the right side of your recorded video.

- We have some default backgorunds: Apple, Nature, Abstract, Tech, Minimalist

## Adjust the Video Frame

You can zoom in or out of your video frame and customize its corner radius.

Scroll down in the editor settings to find additional frame options, including:

- **Zoom**, you can set:
  - Minimum 50%: Shows more background
  - Maximum 100%: Background will not be visible. 

![SCR 20260106 Ntbl 2](/images/SCR-20260106-ntbl-2.png)

- **Corner Radius**\
  Controls how rounded the corners of your recorded video appear.

## Border Video Frame

You can add a border to your video frame for a more polished look.

Turn on Enable Border, then customize it using the following options:

- Border Width: Adjust the thickness of the border.
- Border Style: Choose from Solid, Gradient, or Glow.
- Border Color: Select the color that best matches your video style.
