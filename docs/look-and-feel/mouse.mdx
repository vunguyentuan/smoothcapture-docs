## Cursor Settings

Smooth Capture gives you full control over how the cursor appears and behaves in your recordings, helping viewers follow your actions more easily.

![SCR 20260106 Nxlq](/images/SCR-20260106-nxlq.png)

### Visibility

- **Show Cursor**\
  Toggle this on to display the cursor in your recording.
- **Auto-hide When Idle**\
  Automatically fades out the cursor when it stops moving, keeping the screen clean and distraction-free.

---

### Motion

- **Smooth Motion** _(Recommended)_\
  Applies cinematic smoothing with smart easing for more natural cursor movement.
- **Classic Motion**\
  Keeps the original cursor path without smoothing.

---

### Motion Blur

**Enable Motion Blur: **Adds a subtle blur trail when the cursor moves quickly, enhancing motion clarity.

---

### Click Feedback

**Click Ripple: **Displays a gentle ripple effect whenever you click, making interactions easier to follow.

### Size

**Scale: **Customize the size of your mouse
